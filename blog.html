My Blog

The Journey of a Footballer: From Kathmandu to the World

Football has been more than just a sport for me; it’s been a way of life. Growing up in Kathmandu, my passion for football was nurtured at an early age. From playing in the dusty streets with my friends to representing my school and eventually joining professional leagues, every step has been a lesson in dedication, perseverance, and love for the game.

As I look back, the journey has been filled with challenges, but each one has shaped me into the player I am today. Whether it’s the thrill of scoring a goal, the heartbreak of a missed opportunity, or the joy of celebrating a victory with my team, football continues to teach me valuable life lessons. This blog is a space where I share these experiences, insights, and the many stories that football has gifted me.

Beyond the Pitch: My Love for Art and Travel

While football takes up most of my time, I believe in living a well-rounded life, and for me, that includes art and travel. Drawing has always been a form of expression where I can explore my creativity. Whether it’s sketching a landscape or capturing the intricate details of a portrait, art allows me to see the world through a different lens.

Traveling, on the other hand, fuels my curiosity. From the bustling streets of Barcelona to the serene beaches of Bali, each destination offers new cultures to explore and experiences to cherish. These adventures not only refresh my mind but also provide inspiration for both my game and my art. I hope to use this blog to document these journeys, sharing the beauty and diversity of the world with you.
